# Project

Infrastructure supporting the investigation. This directory holds working material, administrative artefacts, and the active edge of research development. Theoretical content lives in [1-theory/](../1-theory/); project-level work lives here.

## Contents

**[working-papers/](working-papers/)** ([README](working-papers/README.md)): the active development workspace.

Three subdirectories organise the work:

- **proposals/** tracks doctoral research proposal iterations. The original Oxford DPhil proposal (v1, manifesto-framed, Ruth Chang targeted) is retained as a historical artefact. The current LSE MPhil/PhD proposal (v2, narrowed to the structural hypothesis, Alexander + Bradley targeted) is the live document, pre-submission.
- **reading/** holds the primary-source research log and per-paper reading notes. The bibliography tracks priority, status and access across the coordination-theory, decision-theory and evolutionary-game-theory literatures the thesis engages with.
- **writing/** contains synthesis essays and cross-cutting working documents, including the philosophical-tensions tracker (documenting unresolved tensions between the broader Unity Compass repository and the narrower v2 thesis) and the foundational logos essay.

## Currently Absent

Earlier iterations of this directory included placeholders for community infrastructure, critique responses, audience-specific entry points, style metadata, and external resources. These were pruned during the v2 alignment pass. Their content was aspirational and unfulfilled, and maintaining empty placeholders was creating architectural promises the work had not earned. They may return when there is content worth shipping into them.

## Relationship to the Rest of the Repository

This directory is where the investigation's formal research gets developed. The [theory spine](../1-theory/) makes the public argument. The working papers here are where the PhD-level formal work is scaffolded, the reading is tracked, and the distance between the public argument and the formal thesis is worked out honestly.

## Status

Active. `working-papers/` is currently the only populated subdirectory. The rest of `3-project/` will grow as the project matures and as new forms of infrastructure become worth building.
