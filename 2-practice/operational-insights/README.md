# Operational Insights

Service-specific observations from the author's operational experience, written with explicit ties to the theory claims they ground, extend or complicate. Where [reflections/](../reflections/) hold broad thematic thinking, this directory holds bounded observations anchored to particular coordination problems from lived service.

## Purpose

The investigation's formal claims rest partly on the author's operational background: nuclear reactor operations on submarines, AUKUS-adjacent defence consulting, and cross-institutional project work across military, commercial and consulting contexts. That background is one of the investigation's comparative advantages over other philosophical projects on coordination theory.

This directory is where that experience becomes explicit content, tied to specific theoretical commitments made elsewhere in the repository. Each insight answers: what did operational experience show, and how does it support or complicate the theory claim?

## Convention

**Filename pattern**: `domain-topic-slug.md` (e.g. `nuclear-operations-safety-culture.md`, `defence-consulting-bureaucratic-friction.md`, `aukus-coordination-across-strategic-doctrines.md`).

**Structure** (looser than case studies but consistent):

- **Context**: what domain, what timeframe, what role (as far as can be disclosed)
- **Observation**: what the author actually saw or experienced, at whatever level of specificity is shareable
- **Where it ties to theory**: which archetype, which failure mode, which design principle, which current approach it supports or complicates
- **Open questions**: what this operational view doesn't resolve, or what new questions it raises

**Voice**: first-person, honest, bounded by clearance and confidentiality obligations. Where specific details cannot be shared, the pattern or dynamic usually can be. Where neither can be shared, the observation probably doesn't belong here.

**Dating and revising**: entries carry their written date. If operational perspective shifts over time (as it reliably does), add dated addenda rather than overwriting.

## What Belongs Here

- Specific incidents, patterns or dynamics from operational experience that inform theory claims
- Reflections on hierarchical structure, safety culture, political pressure, bureaucratic friction, cross-institutional coordination dynamics
- Observations of failure modes and success patterns from the inside
- Grounds for the author's claim that certain formal assumptions (about rationality, commensurability, iteration) don't survive contact with operational reality

## What Doesn't Belong Here

- Broad thematic essays without specific grounding (those go in [reflections/](../reflections/))
- Analytical case studies of public events (those go in [1-theory/02-landscape/case-studies/](../../1-theory/02-landscape/case-studies/))
- Anything that would violate clearance, NDA or confidentiality obligations

## Constraints

Operational content here respects clearance and confidentiality obligations. Where specific details cannot be shared, general patterns and dynamics often can be. The author is responsible for judging what crosses the line.
