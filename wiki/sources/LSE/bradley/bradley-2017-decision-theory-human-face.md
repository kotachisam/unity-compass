# Decision Theory with a Human Face

Bradley's magnum opus. Brings together work on decision under ambiguity, belief revision and social choice. The pre-publication manuscript (April 2016) is hosted on his LSE page; page numbers may differ slightly from the published CUP edition.

## Why it matters

This is the comprehensive framework the investigation's sub-question 3 (rationality under value incommensurability) needs to engage with. Target chapters: ambiguity, social choice, decision under severe uncertainty. The question is whether Bradley's apparatus can be extended from impartial evaluation to situated coordination among agents with incommensurable values.
