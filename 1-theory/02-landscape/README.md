# Landscape

The [core documents](../01-core/) established *what* is broken and *why*. This directory maps *how coordination actually works in the wild* — where it succeeds, where it fails, what others are doing about it, and what the evidence looks like up close.

## Reading Order

**[Top-Down and Bottom-Up](01-top-down-bottom-up.md)** — The foundational pattern. Most coordination flows downward from authority. It's good at execution and bad at knowing what to execute. Bottom-up coordination surfaces intelligence brilliantly but struggles to mobilise action. What's needed is a surgical combination of both. Examples from open-source, religion, military command and scientific research.

**[Where It Works](02-where-it-works.md)** — Coordination across genuine difference does happen. Six recurring success factors extracted from cases spanning interpersonal relationships to international treaties: shared stakes without shared ideology, distributed intelligence with institutional scaffolding, iterative trust-building, mechanisms for self-correction, transparency of mechanism and preservation of exit.

**[Where It Fails](03-where-it-fails.md)** — Seven recurring failure modes: speed mismatch, information distortion, accountability void, capture dynamics, legitimacy deficit, false consensus and identity entrenchment. Each illustrated across scales from kitchen sinks to global finance. These failure modes compound, and together they describe what the institutional barriers from [Why Not Already](../01-core/03-why-not-already.md) look like in practice.

**[Current Approaches](04-current-approaches.md)** — An honest survey of who else is working on coordination across difference, organised by scale: interpersonal (NVC, restorative justice, Gottman), organisational (sociocracy, Agile, Theory U, Quaker consensus), community (Ostrom, Indigenous governance), national (citizens' assemblies, quadratic voting, digital democracy), international (multilateral treaties, polycentric governance) and cross-scale (the metacrisis framework, complexity science, game theory extensions). Where the Unity Compass sits in relation to all of them.

**[Case Studies](05-case-studies/)** — Detailed analytical examinations of specific coordination successes, failures and works-in-progress. These are the evidence base that the analytical documents above draw on.

## Relationship to the Rest of the Repository

This directory provides the empirical grounding. The [core](../01-core/) makes the philosophical argument. The landscape shows what the world actually looks like. The [frameworks](../03-frameworks/) propose what to build differently. The [practice spine](../../2-practice/) explores what coordination across difference feels like from the inside.
