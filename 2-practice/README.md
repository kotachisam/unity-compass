# Practice

The experiential and operational dimension of the investigation. Primarily a thinking log. Secondarily a place where lived experience of coordination across difference gets tied to the theoretical claims developed in [1-theory/](../1-theory/).

## What This Is, Now

In the v2-aligned investigation, this directory is not a separate "practice spine" making parallel claims to the theory spine. It is the author's working log of reflections, observations and operational insights that inform the formal thinking and sometimes pull against it.

The primary audience is future Sam: a place to capture thinking as it forms, to test whether observations from operational experience hold up over time, and to maintain continuity across the investigation's slow-burn development.

The secondary audience is anyone else: readers who want texture alongside the formal argument, practitioners looking for honest reflection from someone who has coordinated under catastrophic uncertainty for a living, philosophers wanting empirical grounding for the theory claims.

## Contents

**[reflections/](reflections/)** ([README](reflections/README.md)): first-person essays and thinking-log entries. Thematic rather than case-based. Vulnerable prose where warranted, analytical where that serves better. The thinking log's primary home.

**[operational-insights/](operational-insights/)** ([README](operational-insights/README.md)): service-specific observations from nuclear reactor operations, AUKUS-adjacent defence consulting and cross-institutional project work, written with explicit ties to the theory claims they ground or complicate. Security and confidentiality boundaries respected.

## What's Deferred

The original v0.1 plans included an exercises / experiential-engagement dimension (structured practices, protocols, things readers could actually try). That's been set aside until the author has sat with what, specifically, that dimension should contain in the v2-aligned work.

Contemplative or Eastern-philosophy content was part of earlier repository thinking. It may eventually find a home here (where first-person voice is acceptable), or may stay out of the project entirely. Not yet decided.

## Convention

Content here is honest-in-progress, not polished for publication. Rough edges, uncertain conclusions, reversals of earlier thinking are all welcome and expected. Entries should be dated so the trajectory of thinking stays legible.

## Status

Early. Single-author thinking log in current phase. Structure likely to evolve as content accumulates and the author discovers what this dimension actually wants to hold.

## Relationship to the Rest of the Repository

The [theory spine](../1-theory/) makes the formal argument. This directory is where the operational experience that informs and tests the formal argument lives in first-person voice. Formal research and doctoral proposal development happen in [3-project/working-papers/](../3-project/working-papers/).
